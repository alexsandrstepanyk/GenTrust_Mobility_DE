### 2. **Dignity Score Formula** (200+ lines) - Anti-Fraud System

```
DS = Σ(V_ai × P_urban) + Σ(Q_done × T_speed) - F_penalty
```

✅ **Components:**
- V_ai: AI Confidence (0.1-1.0) from Gemini
- P_urban: Priority weight (Hole=10, Graffiti=2, Trash=3)
- Q_done: Quest base rewards (Delivery=20, Report=10)
- T_speed: Speed multiplier (<1h = 1.5x, <24h = 1.2x)
- F_penalty: Anti-fraud penalties (-50 for fakes, -20 for delays)

✅ **Level System:**
- Level 1-10: Observer (👀)
- Level 11-20: Assistant (🙋)
- Level 21-30: Activist (⚡)
- Level 31-40: Guardian (🛡️)
- Level 41-50: Urban Legend (👑)

✅ **Complete Example:**
- Maxi finds critical pothole (V_ai=0.98 × P_urban=10 = 10 points)
- Completes delivery in 45 minutes (20 × 1.5 = 30 points)
- Total: 40 points = +4 levels = Guardian title unlock
